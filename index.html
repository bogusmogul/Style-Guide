Welcome to the Department of Education Government Agency Guidelines Page

This style guide for the Department of Education aims to provide guidelines on maintaining cohesive branding across multiple platforms: Web, Tablet, and Mobile.

Typography

DoED uses the following two typefaces:

IBM Plex Serif

IBM Plex Serif is to be used for headers.

Noto Sans

Noto Sans is to be used for paragraphs and small text.
